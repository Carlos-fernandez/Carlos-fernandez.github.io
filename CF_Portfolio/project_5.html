Torsional Encoder Wear Test Fixture
Brief:

The Makerbot Z18 demonstrated excess wear on the smart extruder(+) encoder wheel after x amount of hours due to sharp filament routing paths. The goal of this fixture was to introduce the worst bend radius compared to the other printers. The fixture utilizes a stepper motor, coupled with a shaft and three timing pulleys linked with a timing belt that controls the rotation of the extruder carriages. The smart extruder mounts on the carriage that was designed and 3D printed. The motor will rotate all three carriage simultaneously by 45 degrees.

Development:
  • Design moving reference frame that accelerates wear on the encoder wheel the smart extruder +.
  • Ultilize 80/20 Aluminum extrusions to setup test.
  • Construct the test fixture with components in the Mechanical Laboratory.
  • Write a test script that controls the feed rate and rotating angle using in house electronics.
