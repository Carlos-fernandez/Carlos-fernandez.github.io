Automated Seed Planter
Brief:

I have undertaken a personal project aimed at developing an automated vehicle designed specifically for planting beans in a grid pattern, catering to agricultural needs. This innovative robot is equipped with zero-point turning capability, enabling efficient mobility within the designated grid pattern. The project involves the creation of over 20 custom parts, with a significant emphasis on utilizing 3D printing technology to fabricate these components. By leveraging custom-designed parts and incorporating advanced mobility features, the automated vehicle promises to revolutionize the bean planting process, enhancing efficiency and productivity in the agricultural sector.

Development:
  • Develop a vehicle with the capability to navigate through soil terrain in a predetermined grid pattern.
  • Create an efficient digging mechanism that can effectively burrow holes into the soil for planting.
  • Design a versatile seed dispenser capable of handling various types of seeds.
  • Develop the necessary electronics system to power the vehicle, including four drive wheels, one stepper motor, three servos, and a drill motor.
  • Compose a parametric test script that accommodates different grid sizes for planting, ensuring flexibility and adaptability in the planting process.
