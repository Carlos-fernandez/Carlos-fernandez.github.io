Makerbot Replicator Mini +, Gantry Tester
Brief:

The Replicator mini had Y axis linear issues. There were cases that the Y motor that drives the gantry + the extruder carriage would skip, resulting into misalign layers on print quality. The mini gantry tester was to understand the assembly process to eliminate high frictional forces on the mechanical system.

Development:
  • Use a force gauge to measure frictional force to move the Y axis Gantry.
  • Use an external linear system that pulls and pushes the gantry on the Y axis.
  • Ensure that the printer is secured in a tightly manner.
  • Provide a force specification to create a PASS/FAIL Creterion.
