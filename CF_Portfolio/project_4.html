Makerbot Replicator Mini +, Gantry Tester
Brief:

The Replicator Mini experienced Y-axis linear issues, with instances where the Y motor driving the gantry and extruder carriage would occasionally skip, causing misaligned layers and impacting print quality. To address this, the Mini Gantry Tester was developed to gain insights into the assembly process and identify potential causes of high frictional forces within the mechanical system. The ultimate goal of the tester was to mitigate these issues and ensure optimal performance and print quality.

Development:
  • Utilize a force gauge to accurately measure the frictional force required to move the Y-axis gantry.
  • Implement an external linear system that applies controlled pulling and pushing forces on the gantry along the Y-axis.
  • Ensure that the printer is securely fastened to minimize any potential movement during testing.
  • Establish a force specification that serves as the basis for determining a PASS/FAIL criterion, enabling effective evaluation of the test results.
